\section{Data Model}

PaperRacks separates intellectual identity from physical files.

\begin{longtable}{p{0.28\textwidth}p{0.62\textwidth}}
\textbf{Entity} & \textbf{Purpose} \\
Work & Conceptual scholarly paper. \\
WorkVersion & Specific version such as arXiv v1, accepted manuscript, or publisher version. \\
File & Physical file identity, usually a PDF. \\
FileSegment & Page range or internal segment when one file contains multiple papers. \\
FileWorkLink & Many-to-many relationship between files and works. \\
Location & Server path, managed object, URL, or agent-owned file reference. \\
Shelf & Collection of works. A work may appear in many shelves. \\
Rack & Collection of shelves. A shelf may appear in many racks. \\
Reference & Bibliographic item extracted from a work. \\
CitationMention & In-text citation marker and context sentence/coordinates. \\
MetadataAssertion & Provenance-aware candidate metadata value. \\
Summary & Extracted abstract, local AI summary, external AI summary, or human summary. \\
AuditEvent & Authentication, browsing, export, import, and change log. \\
\end{longtable}

The model must allow one file to contain multiple works and one work to have multiple files or versions.
