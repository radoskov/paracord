## Goal

Bibliography references rarely link to a library paper even when the cited paper is genuinely local,
because titles differ by punctuation/dash/case (e.g. "KnowRob: A knowledge processing…" locally vs.
"KnowRob – A Knowledge Processing…" cited). The reference then shows "external" and its **Import**
creates a near-duplicate `Work`. Batch 12 adds a tolerant title(+year+author) matcher that surfaces
**"likely local"** candidates the owner confirms/rejects, plus the canonical-reference refactor that
makes matching run once per cited thing and apply to every citing work.

## Phase 1 — what changed (model + read paths)

- `backend/app/models/citation.py`: `Reference` gains `suggested_work_id`, `match_score`,
  `normalized_title`, `authors` (JSONB), `dedup_key` (String(512), indexed, **non-unique**); loses
  `citing_work_id`/`source_tei_id`; gains a `citations` relationship. New `ReferenceCitation`

- `backend/app/services/reference_links.py` (new): `reference_dedup_key(...)`,
  `find_or_create_reference(...)`, `citing_work_ids_subquery(...)`, `references_for_work(...)`. Dedup

  `extraction.py` (unlink this work's links, find-or-create canonical refs, prune orphans),
  `reference_graph.py`, `citation_graph.py`, `citation_summary.py`, `works.py`
  (`cites:`/`cited_by_local:`/`has_references`, work-delete orphan prune, `list_work_references`),
